😉 About

I am now a PhD student at Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), majoring in mechanical engineering. My supervisor is Prof. Jinglong Chen. Up to now, I have published 9 SCI papers as the first author and more than 20 co-authored papers. I've also served for ISA, KBS, JMS and other journals as a reviewer for many times.

Currently, My research interests focus on

  • Data-driven mechanical fault diagnosis
  • Mechanical signal processing
  • Mechanical Anomaly detection
